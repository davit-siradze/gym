Ambri as an online trainer

Ambri - a hero in Georgian mythology, who is distinguished by enormous strength, height and weight. In the cycle of stories about Amirani, Ambri is associated with the motive of "burying him alive": a still alive Ambri leads twelve pairs of oxen to the burial site, his foot falling from a cart on the way, plowing the ground like a plow. The latter makes it possible to assume that the face of the amber originated in the farming population.
